## Tips for Faster Responses

1. **Send to 20+ investors at a time** — batch outreach
2. **Segment by buy box** — don't send a $125K SFH to someone who only buys $300K+ multi-families
3. **Lead with the numbers** — "ARV $300K, price $125K" hits harder than the address
4. **Create urgency** — "Another buyer is looking at it today" = faster responses
5. **Follow up 3x** — text, call, text. Most deals die at the first no-response
6. **Attach the deal sheet** when they respond — one-pager with all the math

## Where to Find Buyers FAST

1. **PropStream** — pull cash investors by zip code, export list, text/email blast
2. **BiggerPockets** — post in forums, DM active investors
3. **Facebook Groups** — "Philadelphia Real Estate Investors" etc.
4. **Local REIA meetings** — meet in person, cards
5. **Your own network** — anyone who's talked about real estate investing in the last 2 years
6. **Title company referrals** — ask title reps who buys cash in your area
