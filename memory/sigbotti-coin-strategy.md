## 1. What Separates the $1M+ Coins from the $2-5K Deaths

### The Hard Truth
- **98.6% of Pump.fun tokens are scams, rugs, or total failures** (per Solidus Labs data)
- Over 7 million tokens launched; only ~97,000 ever held $1K+ liquidity
- The gap between winners and losers is NOT luck — it's prep, narrative, and community velocity

### What Winners Have That Losers Don't

| Factor | Coin that Made It ($1M+) | Coin that Died ($2-5K) |
|---|---|---|
| **Narrative** | Relatable meme or cultural moment | Generic "utility token" or vague name |
| **Visual Identity** | Strong mascot/logo instantly recognizable | Generic copy-paste image |
| **Social Momentum** | Viral TikTok challenge or X hype cycle | Quiet launch, no buzz |
| **KOL Early Buys** | Top traders ape in early, signal credibility | No influencer traction |
| **Community Energy** | Telegram/Discord builds before launch | No pre-existing community |
| **Timing** | Caught a trend or created one | Launched into a crowded no-moment |
| **Name** | Immediately memeable, easy to shill | Forgettable or hard to pronounce |

### The One Thing That Matters Most
**Social velocity.** A coin can have a great name and even decent art, but if it doesn't hit social media in the first 30-60 minutes after launch, it's already dead. Pump.fun's bonding curve means early buyers get in cheap — so social momentum = price momentum = visibility.

## 2. Most Successful Pump.fun Launch Strategies (2024-2026)

### Strategy A: The Viral Animal / Character Play
**Examples:** MOODENG (baby hippo, $170M in 3 days), PNUT (squirrel, $1.8B peak)
- Pick an animal or character already going viral on social media
- Ride the existing trend momentum
- Low lift — the meme is already proven

### Strategy B: The TikTok Challenge Drop
**Examples:** CHILLGUY (laid-back dog, $543M — fueled by the #CHILLGUYChallenge)
- Create a challenge or meme format that users can participate in
- TikTok video drives awareness → investors ape in
- Key: the challenge has to be REPLICABLE by others

### Strategy C: The AI Agent Narrative
**Examples:** GOAT (AI memecoin, $1.3B peak — first Pump.fun token to hit $1B)
- Position the coin at the intersection of AI hype + meme culture
- "AI is buying this coin" narrative generates intrigue
- Works especially well with the degen trader crowd

### Strategy D: The Celebrity / Influencer Pump
**Examples:** MOTHER (Iggy Azalea, $200M peak)
- Get an established public figure to promote the coin
- Their existing audience becomes the launch community
- Risk: depends entirely on the celebrity's involvement

### Strategy E: The Anti-Narrative / Edgy Meme
**Examples:** FARTCOIN ($1B at peak)
- Deliberately absurdist, no-utility coin
- "This is stupid and that's the point" energy
- Works when it leans ALL the way into irony
