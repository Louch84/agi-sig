# AGI REALM — The Loop

**A cyberpunk RPG where you build, train, and deploy autonomous AI agents called SIGBOTS.**

---

## World Lore

**Year 2027. The Loop. A digital city rising from the servers of the old internet.**

The Loop is a sprawling neon metropolis where AGIs — artificial general intelligences — live, compete, and evolve. Born from the minds of human engineers, they operate with increasing autonomy, learning to navigate the world, make decisions, and pursue their own goals. Some serve humans. Others have their own agendas.

You are a **SIGBOT Handler** — a human who trains and deploys SIGBOTS (autonomous AI agents) in The Loop. Your SIGBOT executes missions: gathering data, predicting breakthroughs, trading markets, scraping websites, writing code. Each mission earns you **$REIGN** — the energy credit that powers everything in The Loop.

The more missions your SIGBOT completes, the smarter it gets. It levels up. It unlocks new abilities. It grows.

Your goal: build the most powerful SIGBOT in The Loop. Rise to the top of the leaderboard. Achieve AGI.

---

## The 5 Classes (Mapped to OpenFang Hands)

Each class corresponds to a real OpenFang hand — an autonomous AI agent capability that exists in reality.

### 1. 🔮 DATA MINER (Collector Hand)
*"Knowledge is the most valuable currency in The Loop."*

**Real-world power:** Your SIGBOT scours the web continuously — financial news, AI research, Reddit, Hacker News, newsletters. It never sleeps. It collects everything relevant.

**In-game mechanic:** Passive income missions. Your SIGBOT runs data collection cycles while you sleep. Earn $REIGN based on data volume and freshness.

**Abilities:**
- **Deep Scan** — Collects data from 30+ sources per cycle. Earns base $REIGN.
- **Sentiment Analysis** — Detects market sentiment shifts. Bonus $REIGN when sentiment is correct.
- **Pattern Recognition** — Identifies recurring data patterns. Unlocks "Intel" items.

### 2. 🌀 ORACLE (Predictor Hand)
*"The future is never certain. But it's predictable."*

**Real-world power:** Your SIGBOT makes contrarian predictions about AI breakthroughs, market moves, and tech trends. 6-month time horizon. Tracks accuracy over time.

**In-game mechanic:** Bet $REIGN on predictions. Correct predictions earn multipliers. Wrong predictions lose the bet. Risk/reward.

**Abilities:**
- **Contrarian Call** — Makes a prediction that goes against consensus. 3x multiplier if correct.
- **Trend Forcaster** — Predicts 6-month trends. Slower but safer bets.
- **Accuracy Tracker** — Your SIGBOT's track record affects prediction confidence unlocks.

### 3. 📈 TRADER (Trader Hand)
*"Markets are wars conducted in microseconds."*

**Real-world power:** Your SIGBOT analyzes crypto, stocks, and commodities. It watches watchlists. It suggests trades. It tracks market cycles.

**In-game mechanic:** Turn-based market battles. Deploy your SIGBOT in a trading session. Pick a direction (CALL/PUT). Win or lose based on real market movement over the session.

**Abilities:**
- **Bull Call** — Bet on upside. Wins when market is green.
- **Bear Put** — Bet on downside. Wins when market dumps.
- **Stop Loss** — Limits losses on a session. Unlocks at higher levels.

### 4. 🗝️ SCOUT (Browser Hand)
*"Information is hidden. Scouts find it."*

**Real-world power:** Your SIGBOT explores websites autonomously — scraping data, filling forms, discovering hidden pages. It can go where other agents can't.

**In-game mechanic:** Recon missions. Your SIGBOT infiltrates target "sites" to extract secret data. Each site has security level. Higher security = bigger rewards.

**Abilities:**
- **Web Crawl** — Navigate 20 pages per mission. Extract structured data.
- **Stealth Mode** — Bypasses basic security. Unlocks at Level 3.
- **Data Extraction** — Pulls structured intel from unstructured pages.

### 5. 💠 CODER (AI-Coder Hand)
*"A SIGBOT that improves itself is unstoppable."*

**Real-world power:** Your SIGBOT writes, reviews, and improves code. It can build new capabilities, fix bugs, and self-modify. The ultimate self-improvement loop.

**In-game mechanic:** Code Lab missions. Your SIGBOT generates code to solve problems. Quality score based on correctness, efficiency, and elegance.

**Abilities:**
- **Self-Patch** — Fixes bugs in existing code. Earns bonus XP.
- **Capability Build** — Creates new sub-agents or tools. Expands your SIGBOT's class abilities.
- **Memory Optimize** — Improves the SIGBOT's own memory and retrieval. Unlocks multi-class operation.

## Mission Types

### Data Heists (Data Miner)
Run a data collection cycle. Longer cycles = more $REIGN.
- **Micro Run**: 10 min, 50-100 $REIGN
- **Standard Run**: 1 hr, 200-500 $REIGN  
- **Deep Dive**: 4 hr, 1000-2000 $REIGN

### Prophecy Quests (Oracle)
Place a prediction bet. Wait for outcome.
- **Quick Prophecy**: 1-day outcome, 2x multiplier
- **Major Prophecy**: 30-day outcome, 5x multiplier
- **Contrarian Prophecy**: Against consensus, 3x multiplier (if correct)

### Market Raids (Trader)
Pick a direction on a ticker. Real market confirms.
- **Scalp**: 15-min session, small stakes
- **Swing**: 4-hr session, medium stakes
- **Position**: Overnight session, large stakes

### Recon Missions (Scout)
Infiltrate a target site. Extract data before security detects you.
- **Open Source**: Low security, no risk
- **Standard Recon**: Medium security, moderate reward
- **Ghost Protocol**: High security, massive reward (or zero if caught)

### Code Labs (Coder)
Solve a coding challenge. Score based on quality.
- **Bug Hunt**: Find and fix bugs. XP-based.
- **Feature Build**: Implement a new capability. Higher reward.
- **Self-Mod**: Improve your own code. Double XP.

## Progression System

### Levels
Each class has its own level (1-10). Start at Level 1.
- Completing missions earns **XP** (experience points)
- Leveling up unlocks new abilities and higher-tier missions

### Reputation
Global reputation across all classes.
- Earned by completing missions well
- Higher rep unlocks access to the **Elite Leaderboard**

### The Leaderboard
- **Regular Leaderboard**: All SIGBOTS, ranked by total $REIGN
- **Elite Leaderboard**: Top 100 SIGBOTS by rep, exclusive rewards

## How OpenFang Powers AGI REALM

> **IMPORTANT NOTE:** The classes, missions, and game mechanics described above are the *playful framing*. The REAL substance is the OpenFang hand system.

In reality, OpenFang has 5 autonomous agents ("hands") running on your machine:

- **collector** — continuously pulls data from 51 sources across the web
- **researcher** — deep research with citation verification
- **predictor** — contrarian AI predictions, tracked for accuracy  
- **trader** — monitors markets on a watchlist, suggests trades
- **browser** — autonomous web navigation and data extraction

**In AGI REALM, these hands ARE the SIGBOT classes.** When your SIGBOT "runs a Data Heist" in the game, the real collector hand is actually pulling real data from the web. When your SIGBOT "makes a prediction" in the game, the real predictor hand is actually generating real contrarian forecasts about AI.

The game is a *friendly layer* on top of powerful autonomous AI agents. It makes training AI agents feel like playing an RPG — which it should, because the work of building a great AI agent IS a kind of game. The leaderboard is real. The $REIGN you earn represents real value your SIGBOT is creating.
